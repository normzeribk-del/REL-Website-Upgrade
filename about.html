About Rumbam Engineers

Our Story

Over 30 Years of Engineering Excellence

Rumbam Engineers Ltd has a well-respected professional history since 1994, which is reflected in the many and varied engineering projects successfully completed throughout Papua New Guinea. We are recognized by our peers as a leading nationally owned engineering consultancy firm, providing professional services in a number of engineering and building disciplines.

Starting out in civil engineering design, documentation, and project management of roads, subdivisions and drainage, we have grown into a competitive multi-disciplinary professional engineering consultancy. Today, REL has in-house capacity to provide consultancy in civil engineering, transportation, aviation, water resources/waste water and environmental engineering, architecture and building, and project management services.

We have forged lifelong ties with other professionals who have played a key role in our success. Our client base extends from individuals to government agencies and the corporate sector. We are currently located on Level One, Suite Two, RDF House, Jacksons Parade, 7 Mile, Port Moresby, NCD.

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To deliver professional, sustainable, and reliable engineering solutions that meet the highest standards of technical excellence, safety, and environmental responsibility, while contributing to the development and prosperity of Papua New Guinea.

Our Vision

To be the leading engineering consultancy in Papua New Guinea, recognized for our integrity, professionalism, and ability to deliver innovative solutions that support national development and international projects in PNG.

Our Commitment

We are committed to delivering technically robust engineering solutions tailored to PNG's unique conditions, ensuring client satisfaction through professional, timely, and cost-effective services while promoting sustainable development.
