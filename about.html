About Rumbam Engineers

Our Story

Three Decades of Engineering Excellence

Rumbam Engineers Limited (REL) was established in March 1994 as a fully Papua New Guinea–owned civil engineering consulting firm. Over three decades, we have grown into a trusted partner for government, private sector, donors, and international organisations operating in PNG.

From our headquarters in Port Moresby, we have undertaken projects in urban, rural, and remote locations, successfully addressing the unique engineering challenges of Papua New Guinea's diverse terrain and climate.

We deliver innovative, practical, and sustainable engineering solutions that are technically robust, socially responsible, and aligned with international best practices.

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To deliver professional, sustainable, and reliable engineering solutions that meet the highest standards of technical excellence, safety, and environmental responsibility, while contributing to the development and prosperity of Papua New Guinea.

Our Vision

To be the leading engineering consultancy in Papua New Guinea, recognized for our integrity, professionalism, and ability to deliver innovative solutions that support national development and international projects in PNG.

Our Commitment

We are committed to delivering technically robust engineering solutions tailored to PNG's unique conditions, ensuring client satisfaction through professional, timely, and cost-effective services while promoting sustainable development.
