6. Architecture & Building

REL provides consultancy in Architecture and Building, having completed design and documentation of buildings, received statutory approvals, completed tender processes, produced contract documents and supervised contracts to successful completion across Papua New Guinea including Wewak, Madang, Kavieng, Boroko, Kanudi, Gordons and the Ela Beach waterfront in NCD.

Houses, Flats & Townhouses Commercial & Retail Buildings Hotels, Resorts & Public Buildings Urban Renewal & Redevelopment

Why REL? Registered architects with PNG Board of Architects, proven track record across multiple provinces, seamless integration with civil and structural engineering disciplines.

7. Marine Engineering

REL has provided for the Government of PNG a Design Review of the Pacific Marine Industrial Zone (PMIZ) in Madang and provided Project Management Services for this large marine infrastructure project. For the National Fisheries Authority, REL completed a Feasibility Study for developing marine and building infrastructure including jetties, finger way structures, floating pontoons, slipways, marine workshops, communication towers and warehouses.

Port & Wharf Design Marine Infrastructure Studies Jetties & Slipways Marine Project Management

Why REL? Deep knowledge of PNG's coastal and marine environments, experienced marine infrastructure specialist with local and Australian work experience.

8. Aviation Engineering

REL has carried out feasibility studies for airport facility relocation in Chimbu Province (Kundiawa to Kerowagi) and provided consultancy for PNG Air Services' Air Transport Management System (ATMS). REL provides comprehensive aviation engineering services from feasibility and design through to construction supervision.

Airport Feasibility Studies Airstrip Design & Supervision Air Transport Systems Aviation Infrastructure

Why REL? Proven experience with PNG's aviation sector, knowledge of remote and provincial airstrip challenges, multi-disciplinary team covering civil, structural and planning needs.

Topographical Surveys Hydrographic Surveys Cadastral & Boundary Surveys Engineering Surveys (Roads & Drainage)
