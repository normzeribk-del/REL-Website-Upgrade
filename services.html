What We Offer

Comprehensive Engineering Solutions

Rumbam Engineers Limited provides end-to-end engineering services across multiple sectors in Papua New Guinea. Our services combine technical expertise, practical solutions, and international best practices to deliver sustainable, safe, and cost-effective outcomes. 1. Civil Engineering Services

We provide end-to-end civil engineering solutions for public and private sector clients, focusing on infrastructure that supports community development and economic growth across Papua New Guinea.

Water Supply & Distribution Systems Sewerage & Wastewater Treatment Flood Control & Drainage Municipal Infrastructure

Why REL for Civil Engineering? Tailored designs for PNG's unique terrain and climate, compliance with local and international standards, and practical solutions for long-term sustainability.

2. Transport & Infrastructure Engineering

Our team specializes in transportation planning and infrastructure development, ensuring safe, efficient, and reliable connectivity across PNG's diverse and challenging terrain.

Roads & Highways Bridges & Culverts Airports & Airstrips Ports, Wharves & Marine Structures

Why REL? Expertise in remote and challenging environments, proven track record with government and industrial clients, commitment to safety, quality, and efficiency.

3. Structural Engineering

REL provides innovative structural engineering solutions for buildings and industrial facilities, emphasising durability, safety, and compliance with both local and international standards.

Commercial & Institutional Buildings Industrial Structures Structural Assessment & Retrofitting Construction Supervision

Why REL? Designs that meet international engineering standards, experienced engineers ensuring structural safety and efficiency, practical solutions suitable for PNG's environmental conditions.

4. Environmental & Utility Systems

We integrate environmental responsibility and sustainability into all engineering projects, ensuring that infrastructure development supports rather than harms PNG's natural environment.

Solid Waste Management Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA) Utility Planning & Design Sustainable Infrastructure Solutions

Why REL? Expertise in environmental engineering in the PNG context, compliance with national regulations and international standards, solutions that balance development with sustainability.

5. Project Management & Advisory Services

We provide professional project management and consultancy to ensure projects are delivered on time, on budget, and to the highest quality — from feasibility through to construction completion.

Feasibility Studies & Planning Engineering Planning & Design Contract Administration Construction Supervision & QC

Why REL? Experienced in complex, multi-disciplinary projects, strong focus on risk management and compliance, delivering efficient, transparent, and accountable project oversight.
