About Rumbam Engineers

Engineering Excellence Since 1994

Rumbam Engineers Ltd is a leading nationally owned engineering consultancy with a well-respected professional history since 1994, recognized by our peers for delivering high-quality engineering solutions across Papua New Guinea. We combine technical expertise with practical, cost-effective approaches to solve today's infrastructure challenges.

Our multi-disciplinary team of engineers, architects, surveyors and project managers work collaboratively to deliver projects in civil engineering, transportation, aviation, marine engineering, architecture, water resources, environmental engineering, structural design, and surveying.

  • Over 30 plus successful projects across multiple sectors
  • Design and engineering solutions
  • ISO-certified quality management processes
  • International experience with local expertise
  • Commitment to sustainable and resilient design
