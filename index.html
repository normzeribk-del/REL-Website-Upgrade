About Rumbam Engineers

A Trusted Engineering Partner in PNG

Rumbam Engineers Limited (REL) is a 100% Papua New Guinea–owned civil engineering consulting firm, established in March 1994. Over three decades, we have built a strong reputation for delivering reliable, sustainable, and cost-effective engineering solutions across a wide range of infrastructure sectors.

We understand the unique engineering challenges of PNG — from difficult terrain and climate conditions to logistical and environmental constraints — and provide practical, context-appropriate solutions that work.

  • Over 30 years of engineering experience in Papua New Guinea
  • PNG-owned and operated, with deep local knowledge
  • Proven capability in complex infrastructure projects
  • Trusted by government, private sector, and development partners
  • Commitment to international standards and best practices
